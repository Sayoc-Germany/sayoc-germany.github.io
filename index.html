About Us

Sayoc Kali is an “All Blades, All the Time” Filipino martial arts system focused on edged weapons combatives, survival and use. Sayoc Kali was founded and created by Pamana Tuhon Christopher C. Sayoc, Sr. Read more about the history and timeline of Sayoc Kali here: sayoc.com/history.

We are the affiliate of Sayoc Kali in Germany since 2016.

Our training groups are located in cities of Munich and Nürnberg.
